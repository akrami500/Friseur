Do you have gift certificates?

Gift cards are generally redeemable only for purchases at the relevant retail premises and can not be cashed out, and in some situations may be subject to an expiry date or fees. Visa and MasterCard credit cards produce generic gift cards which need not be redeemed at particular stores, and which are widely used for cashback marketing strategies. A feature of these cards is that they are generally anonymous and are disposed when the stored value on a card is exhausted.
